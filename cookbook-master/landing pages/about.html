Who are we? What's la cocina?

We are some web-dev students from Germany! La cocina literally means the kitchen in spanish, yup, people in the group share a spanish speaking background and they're integrating all that flavor in this kitchen! Our main belief is that cooking super tasty can at the same time be super healthy! Stay up to date to the blog and enjoy a tasty, healthy, funny way of cooking!
